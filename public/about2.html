Could you be there?

Inhabitant of Vancouver where he may be found seeking the nectars of coffee and meaning.

I am compelled to fill this space, but really, doesn't that sum it up for me. In essence though, there is very little substance to me. I am a collection of influences: everything I utter or think has been uttered or thought before. But If I think that, then why bother write this at all? I write it because, although I am trapped in my own point of view, I like to hope that what I write gets imbued with something more in a reading by you: someone of substance.
